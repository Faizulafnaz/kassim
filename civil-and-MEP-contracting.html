Civil & MEP Contracting Services in Qatar

Successful construction and infrastructure projects rely heavily on high-quality civil and MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) works. At Bin Kasim Business Hub, we provide expert civil and MEP contracting services in Qatar that meet the needs of residential, commercial, industrial, and hospitality sectors. Our experienced team ensures that every project is executed with precision, adherence to local codes, and a commitment to quality and safety.


Our civil contracting services cover all aspects of structural and architectural work, including foundation laying, concrete work, block masonry, plastering, and finishing. Whether it’s a new build or a renovation, we manage the entire civil works lifecycle—from planning and procurement to execution and handover—with strict attention to timelines and budget.


In parallel, our MEP contracting division handles critical building systems that power and sustain modern infrastructure. We provide complete design, installation, and maintenance of HVAC systems, electrical wiring and panels, plumbing networks, fire alarm systems, and drainage solutions. All our work complies with Qatar Construction Standards (QCS) and relevant safety regulations, ensuring operational efficiency and long-term reliability.


We collaborate closely with consultants, engineers, and project owners to deliver turnkey solutions tailored to specific project requirements. With a focus on craftsmanship, innovation, and quality control, Bin Kasim Business Hub ensures your construction project is built to last—with systems that perform flawlessly for years to come.

Bin Kasim offers complete business setup and management solutions in Qatar. With expert knowledge of local regulations and culture, we provide one-stop services for International and local businesses to easily start and run their business in Qatar.
