Trade License Assistance in Qatar

Setting up a business in Qatar involves critical legal steps, and securing a Trade License is one of the most essential. At Bin Kasim Business Services, we provide expert trade license assistance, ensuring your business is registered and operating within Qatar’s legal framework. A Trade License, issued by the Ministry of Commerce and Industry (MOCI), authorizes your business to legally carry out specific commercial activities. Without this license, no business can operate or transact officially in the country.


Our services begin with a detailed consultation, where we evaluate your business activity, structure, and location to determine the correct license category. We then help you with name reservation, obtaining the Commercial Registration (CR), and securing the required approvals from the Municipality (Baladiya), especially regarding your office or business location. Our team also assists in selecting appropriate office spaces that meet licensing requirements and helps with virtual or shared office arrangements where applicable.


We handle all documentation from Articles of Association (AoA) and lease contracts to ID submissions ensuring that everything is prepared accurately and submitted promptly to avoid delays. Beyond the initial license issuance, we also manage license renewals, business activity updates, and modifications like address changes or restructuring, so you stay compliant long-term.


Whether you require a Commercial, Industrial, Professional, or Contracting License, our experienced licensing team offers end-to-end support. With our deep understanding of Qatar’s regulatory environment and strong ties to government departments, we ensure fast-track approvals, transparent processes, and hassle-free execution. Let Bin Kasim Business Services take care of the legal groundwork, so you can focus on growing your business in Qatar with confidence.

Bin Kasim offers complete business setup and management solutions in Qatar. With expert knowledge of local regulations and culture, we provide one-stop services for International and local businesses to easily start and run their business in Qatar.
